Upcoming Activities

We have a "Youth Night" fellowship once a month and it is usually a Friday. The youth also lead the worship the upcoming Sunday after the fellowship.

We welcome you to the NLCF Youth Ministry where we walk faithfully with our youth as they transition from young people to young adults. We strive to engage our teens by organizing monthly fellowships and activities as we recognize their gifts for ministry and questions about spirituality. To get more information about our Youth Ministry, contact our youth administrators.
